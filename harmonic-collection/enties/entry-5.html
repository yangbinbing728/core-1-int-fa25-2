The Aqua Rush

Ride through a river of light and thrill!

The Aqua Rush — Ride through a glowing river of light and motion, where crystal waves rise and fall beneath neon skies. Every drop sends a burst of color into the air, every turn blurs the line between thrill and dream — a rush of water, wind, and pure exhilaration.

Appearance and Structure

The Aqua Rush winds through the lower valley of the park like a living river of glass. Its track glows faintly beneath the water, curving between cliffs of light and mist. Transparent bridges rise and twist above, allowing visitors to watch as the boats shoot past in a shimmer of spray. The main drop towers like a waterfall of light — tall, sleek, and endlessly flowing.

Cabin Design

Inside the boats, the seats are smooth and curved, designed to cradle the rider as the ride speeds forward. A soft hum builds before every drop, rising into a rush of wind and water that fills the air. Mist brushes against your face, cool and sharp, while the scent of ozone and riverlight lingers in your breath. The moments between climbs and falls feel almost weightless — like floating between excitement and calm.

Environment and Atmosphere

Around the river, the air is thick with drifting mist. Holographic fish glide through it, leaving trails of light like swimming comets. Crystal rocks rise from the ground, their edges refracting the moving colors of the ride. Soft blue lights ripple through the fog, guiding the path like an underwater aurora.
