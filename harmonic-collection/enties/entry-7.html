The Ember Drop

A freefall of fire and thrill!

The Ember Drop is not just a freefall — it’s a moment of suspended fire. Rising like a glowing torch in the center of the park, this drop tower mixes fear and beauty into one breathtaking plunge.

Appearance and Structure

The Ember Drop stands tall like a column of molten glass. Its frame glows from within, filled with streams of twisting light. At its peak, the ride pauses for just a heartbeat — long enough for riders to feel weightless.

Material and Light

The tower’s outer shell blends reflective steel with crystal panels. By day, it mirrors the sky; by night, it burns in orange and violet fire. Inside, thin veins of light trace the motion of the rising cabin.

Rider Experience

Riders sit in a circular ring facing outward. A soft vibration hums beneath the seat as the tower climbs. Then — silence. A breath later, the world drops away in a storm of wind and burning color.

Environment and Atmosphere

The tower is surrounded by a shallow reflective pool. Each drop sends shimmering ripples of gold across the surface, turning the ground into a flickering lake of fire and light.
