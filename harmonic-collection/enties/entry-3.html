The Spark Arena

Light, speed, collision — electrified!

Ready, set… crash! The Spark Arena transforms bumper cars into a neon battlefield, where every collision lights up the whole arena in electric colors.

Appearance and Structure

The arena forms a glowing crater in the heart of the park. Its translucent floor reacts to motion — lighting up with every drift, turn, and impact. Overhead, a dome of segmented neon arcs pulses in sync with the music.

Cabin Design

Each car is low, powerful, and wrapped with glowing neon edges. The wheel vibrates gently with movement, and impacts flash in bright colors. Every collision syncs with a beat — turning crashes into music.

Environment and Atmosphere

Glass walls mirror the glowing chaos, creating a looping illusion of infinite motion. Holographic scoreboards float in midair, tracking collisions like sparks in the dark. Mist drifts across the arena floor, scattering the lights into floating ribbons.
