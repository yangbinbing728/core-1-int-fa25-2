Extreme Roller Coaster

Ready for a heart-racing thrill?

This roller coaster delivers the ultimate adrenaline rush with high-speed drops, sharp turns, and intense loops.

Appearance and Structure

The Storm Track rises like a silver skeleton against the sky. Its frame twists and coils in midair, made of shining metal beams that catch every bit of light. From a distance, it looks almost alive — like a giant steel serpent frozen in motion. The track loops over itself several times, forming spirals that seem impossible for gravity to allow. At night, the entire structure glows faintly, as if electricity runs through its veins.

Cabin Design

The passenger cars are shaped like aerodynamic capsules, sleek and sharp-edged, with translucent shells that let riders see the world rushing past. The seats are suspended slightly above the base, creating the illusion of floating. Inside, soft blue light fills the cabin, and small holographic symbols flicker across the dashboard. It feels less like boarding a coaster and more like entering a futuristic aircraft.

Environment and Atmosphere

Around the coaster, artificial mist machines release thin clouds that swirl around the base of the structure. When the lights hit the fog, it creates a glowing storm-like halo — the reason behind its name. Electric sounds echo through hidden speakers: the crackle of thunder, the whoosh of wind. Even before the ride starts, the atmosphere already feels charged, as if a storm is about to break.
