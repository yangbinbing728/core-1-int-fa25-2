The Celestial Carousel

When night falls, the world begins to drift. The Celestial Carousel turns beneath floating lights, each horse glowing softly like starlight.

Appearance and Structure

The Celestial Carousel rises at the center of the park, glowing like a soft red dream. Its layered glass roof spirals upward like a floating crown. Thousands of fiber-light threads sway gently with each rotation.

Cabin Design

Music whispers from hidden speakers — soft piano tones, wind chimes, distant echoes. The platform vibrates gently as it turns, and the air smells faintly of vanilla and warm mist.

Environment and Atmosphere

The carousel is encircled by glowing red trees, their leaves sparkling like holograms. The ground reflects light like a shallow sea of drifting stars, while mist moves quietly across the space.
