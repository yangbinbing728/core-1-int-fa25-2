Solar Spiral Swing

Rising high beneath the golden sky, the Solar Spiral Swing lifts riders into warm sunlight and spinning winds. As the seats sweep outward in a widening spiral, the world below turns into a glowing blur of motion and light.

When the swing reaches full speed, rays of light glint off the metallic arms, scattering golden reflections across the air. You feel weightless—floating, spinning, drifting— as if the sun itself is pulling you into its orbit. For a moment, everything feels warm, bright, and endless.

Appearance and Structure

The Solar Spiral Swing stands tall with a crown-like canopy made of bronze and shimmering glass panels. Thin solar ribbons wrap around the central tower, absorbing sunlight that powers the glowing patterns along the arms. When it spins, the entire structure lights up like a turning sunburst.

Seat and Motion Design

Each seat hangs from golden chains that catch the light as they rise. The swing rotates smoothly at first, then expands outward into a spiraling motion that makes riders feel like they’re drifting through warm currents of air. Soft wind chimes hidden inside the canopy create gentle musical tones as the ride turns.

Environment and Atmosphere

Bright flowers circle the base, moving in the breeze as the swing spins above them. When the sun begins to set, the tower glows amber and pink, casting long, swirling shadows across the ground. Visitors often stop to watch the colors shift—it feels calm, radiant, and alive.
