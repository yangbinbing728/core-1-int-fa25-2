The Luminous Wheel Zone

A dreamlike Ferris wheel experience

Drift through the pastel sky inside a glowing crystal cabin. The wheel turns slowly, giving you a quiet, dreamlike view of the entire amusement park.

Appearance and Structure

The Luminous Wheel stands like a radiant halo at the edge of the park. Its transparent beams catch soft pink and lavender light, making the wheel glow gently at dusk.

Cabin Design

Each cabin is shaped like a floating orb, with 360° clear walls. Soft pastel lights pulse gently inside, creating a calm, airy atmosphere as riders rise into the clouds.

Environment and Atmosphere

Below the wheel, a reflective pool amplifies the glowing colors. Mist drifts across the surface, making the walkway look like a bridge suspended in the sky.
