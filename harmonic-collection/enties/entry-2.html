The Luminous Wheel Zone

A dreamlike Ferris wheel experience

Drift through the sky like a star in motion. The Celestial Orbit takes you high above the park inside crystal cabins glowing with soft blue light.

Appearance and Structure

The Luminous Wheel stands quietly at the edge of the park, taller than any other structure. Its frame isn’t made of ordinary metal — it’s a circle of light, woven from hundreds of transparent beams.

Cabin Design

Inside the cabins, seats curve like petals around a glowing center. Visitors sit facing outward inside 360° transparent walls.

Environment and Atmosphere

The base is surrounded by a shallow reflecting pool. Low mist drifts across the pool, and stepping stones lead to the boarding platform, which glows faintly like a path of starlight.
