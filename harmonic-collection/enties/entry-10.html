Twilight Tea Cups

Spin into a swirl of lights and laughter!

The Twilight Tea Cups spin you into a magical whirl of color and joy. Each cup lifts and rotates, carrying you into a world of sparkling lights and dreamy winds.

Appearance and Structure

The ride features a large circular platform with pastel-colored teacups arranged around a glowing center. Twinkling lanterns hang above, casting playful shadows across the cups, creating the feeling of drifting inside a floating dream.

Cup Design

Each teacup is decorated with shimmering patterns and soft pastel tones. Seats are cushioned and gently elevated, adding to the sense of floating. Riders can spin their cup using a central wheel, creating unique combinations of motion and laughter.

Environment and Atmosphere

Soft mist drifts from hidden fountains, glowing under strands of fairy lights. Gentle melodies accompany the spinning motion, while pastel lights shift in harmony with the rhythm. The whole ride feels whimsical and airy — like stepping inside a swirling pastel dream.
